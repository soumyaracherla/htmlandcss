CAKE RECIPE

    The ingredients:

  • 100g flour
  • 10g sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • salts, pepper

      • Recipe:

  • Mix dry ingredients thoroughly.
  • Pour in wet ingredients.
  • Mix for 10 minuyes.
  • Bake for one hour at 300 degree celcius.

    • Note:

    The recipe may be improved by adding raisins.
